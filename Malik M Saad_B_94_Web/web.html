ALKHIDMANT

Alkhidmat Foundation Pakistan is non-profit organization, fully dedicated to humanitarian services. Alkhidmat's workers and volunteers continue to work tirelessly for the relief of affected people across worldwide.

Edhi Foundation

The Edhi Foundation provides emergency assistance across the entirety of Pakistan and internationally. The foundation provides, among many services, shelter for the destitute, hospitals and medical care, drug rehabilitation services.

Smile Foundation

Smile Foundation is an NGO in India directly benefiting over 750,000 children and their families every year, through more than 350 live welfare projects on education, healthcare, livelihood, and women empowerment, in over 1000 remote villages and slums across 25 states of India.

Sundas Foundation

Sundas Foundation is a Blood Transfusion Center with the aim to provide free of cost & quality comprehensive treatment to patients suffering from Thalassemia, Hemophilia and other blood disorders.
